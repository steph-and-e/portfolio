Thoughts and Reflections

Week 1 Journal

Five years ago, in the eighth grade, I had my first experience coding through Web Development (aside from Scratch). In a personally driven school project, I ventured to learn how to develop a website, enamoured by the concept as a kid. However, due to COVID-19, that project was cut short, and so was my experience with Web Development. Fast-forward to the present day and I am now coming back to what started my passion for coding through COMPSCI 1XD3, a Web Development course at McMaster University. I cannot wait to make my 13-year-old self proud by making my own creative website!

Week 2 Journal

Make no mistake, I am a complete noob at Web Development. Despite WebDev being my first brush against coding, I suck at it. Well, I guess the friendlier way to say that is that I am wayyy more inexperienced with WebDev and front-end development than other software development areas. Even though we're at the end of the UI unit for CS1XD3, I feel like I ought to learn more Web frameworks etc. that I can barely wrap my head around now! But I have hope, that in time, this portfolio will make me proud.
